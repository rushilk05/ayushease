1. AYUSH Startup Challenge

Funding Amount: Up to ₹25 Lakhs
Eligibility: Registered AYUSH startups with innovative products/services
Focus Area: Innovation in traditional medicine, wellness products, and healthcare solutions

Key Features:

  • Seed funding for early-stage startups
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Access to incubation facilities
  • Market linkage support
  • IPR (Intellectual Property Rights) assistance

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Startup must be registered under AYUSH sector
  • Innovative product or service in AYUSH domain
  • Minimum 2 years of operation or proof of concept
  • Compliance with AYUSH regulations

2. National AYUSH Mission

Support Type: Infrastructure and Capacity Building
Target: Strengthening AYUSH healthcare delivery system

Components:

  • Infrastructure Development: Support for setting up clinics, hospitals, and wellness centers
  • Equipment Grants: Subsidized medical equipment and machinery
  • Training Programs: Skill development and capacity building
  • Quality Control: Support for quality assurance and standardization
  • Market Access: Facilitation for market entry and expansion

3. Export Promotion Schemes

Objective: Promote AYUSH products and services in international markets
Benefits: Export incentives, trade fair participation, brand promotion

Available Schemes:

  • Market Access Initiative: Support for international market entry
  • Export Incentives: Financial benefits for export-oriented units
  • Trade Fair Participation: Subsidized participation in international exhibitions
  • Brand Promotion: Marketing and promotional support
  • Certification Support: Assistance with international certifications

4. Research Grants

Focus: Innovation and research in AYUSH sector
Grant Range: ₹5 Lakhs to ₹50 Lakhs based on project scope

Research Areas:

  • Clinical research and validation
  • Product development and standardization
  • Safety and efficacy studies
  • Technology transfer and commercialization
  • Collaborative research projects

Application Requirements:

  • Detailed research proposal
  • Project timeline and budget
  • Team credentials and expertise
  • Expected outcomes and impact

5. Infrastructure Support

Available Support:

  • Land Allocation: Priority allocation in AYUSH parks and clusters
  • Building Grants: Financial support for construction and renovation
  • Equipment Subsidies: Up to 50% subsidy on machinery and equipment
  • Technology Infrastructure: Support for IT and digital infrastructure
  • Utilities: Subsidized power, water, and other utilities

6. Compliance Requirements

Essential Compliance:

  • AYUSH Registration: Valid registration certificate
  • Quality Certifications: GMP, ISO, or equivalent certifications
  • Documentation: Complete business and legal documentation
  • Regular Audits: Compliance with regulatory audits
  • Tax Compliance: GST and other tax registrations

Required Documents:

  • Registration certificate
  • Business license
  • Quality certifications
  • Financial statements
  • Project proposal (for grants)

7. Application Process

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Registration: Register on AYUSHEASE platform
  2. Profile Completion: Complete your startup profile
  3. Scheme Selection: Browse and select suitable schemes
  4. Document Preparation: Gather all required documents
  5. Application Submission: Submit application through platform
  6. Review Process: Application reviewed by authorities
  7. Approval: Receive approval and funding
Important: All applications must be submitted through the AYUSHEASE platform. Ensure all documents are verified and up-to-date before submission.
